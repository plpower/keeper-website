Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add friends?

Go to the Friends tab and search by username or phone number. Once you send a request and they accept, their ratings will appear in your Vetted feed.

Who can see my ratings?

Your ratings are visible only to your friends and your friends' friends (mutuals) within Keeper. They are never public and never visible to strangers.

How does the ranking system work?

When you rate a service, you place it into one of three categories: It's a keeper, Does the job, or Not for me. Within each category, you rank your ratings against each other using a smart algorithm that spreads scores evenly — so your #1 keeper always has a higher score than your #2.

Can I edit or delete a rating?

Yes. Tap into any rated place from your Rated tab and use the edit options to update your notes, provider, or ranking. To remove a rating entirely, contact us at hello@[domain].

Why isn't a salon showing up in search?

Keeper uses Google Places to power search results. If a salon isn't appearing, it may not be listed on Google Maps yet, or the name may be slightly different. Try searching by address or a partial name. If it still doesn't appear, email us and we can look into adding it manually.

How do I report a problem with the app?

Email us at hello@[domain] with a description of the issue, your phone number (so we can find your account), and any screenshots if possible. We take bug reports seriously and aim to resolve issues quickly.

How do I delete my account?

Email us at hello@[domain] with your phone number and a request to delete your account. We'll permanently remove all your data within 30 days and confirm by email.
